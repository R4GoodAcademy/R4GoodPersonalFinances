# HMD life tables

A data frame based on: HMD. Human Mortality Database. Max Planck
Institute for Demographic Research (Germany), University of California,
Berkeley (USA), and French Institute for Demographic Studies (France).
Available at www.mortality.org.

## Usage

``` r
life_tables
```

## Format

### `life_tables`

A data frame with 6 columns:

- country:

  Country name

- sex:

  Sex: "male", "female", "both"

- year:

  Year

- age:

  Age

- mortality_rate:

  Mortality rate

- life_expectancy:

  Life expectancy
